My Top 5 Favorite Movies

  1. Maharaja
    • Year: 2024
    • Main Actors: Vijay Sethupathi, Anurag Kashyap
    • Description: Maharaja is a 2024 Indian Tamil-language action, drama, and thriller film about a barber who seeks revenge after his home is burglarized. The movie stars Vijay Sethupathi as Maharaja, a barber who tells the police that his "lakshmi" has been taken, leaving them unsure if it's a person or object. The film also stars Anurag Kashyap, Mamta Mohandas, Natarajan Subramaniam, Abhirami Gopikumar, Singampuli, Aruldoss, Munishkanth, Sachana Namidass, Manikandan, and Bharathiraja. Nithilan Saminathan directed and co-wrote the script with Raam Murali, and the film is jointly produced by The Route, Think Studios, and Passion Studios.

  2. Kalki 2898 AD
    • Year: 2024
    • Main Actors: Prabhas, Kamal Haasan, Amitabh Bachchan, Deepika Padukone
    • Description: Kalki 2898 AD is a 2024 Indian action, adventure, and drama film that takes place in a post-apocalyptic world in the year 2898 AD. The film is about a group of people on a mission to save the unborn child of lab subject SUM-80, who is the reincarnation of the Hindu god Vishnu, Kalki. The film is inspired by Hindu scriptures and explores themes of hope, survival, and the battle between good and evil. It also weaves together elements of mythology and futuristic science.

  3. Gumraah
    • Year: 2023
    • Main Actors: Aditya Roy Kapur, Mrunal Thakur, Ronit Roy
    • Description: Gumraah is a 2023 Hindi-language action, crime, drama, mystery, and thriller film about a murder investigation that becomes complicated when the police discover two lookalike suspects. The film stars Aditya Roy Kapur in a dual role, along with Mrunal Thakur and Ronit Roy. It was directed by Vardhan Ketkar and written by Magizh Thirumeni, and is a remake of the 2019 Tamil film Thadam.

  4. Tu Jhoothi Main Makkaar
    • Year: 2023
    • Main Actors: Ranbir Kapoor, Shraddha Kapoor, Anubhav Singh Bassi
    • Description: Tu Jhoothi Main Makkaar is a 2023 Hindi-language romantic comedy film that explores the challenges of modern dating. It tells the story of Mickey (Ranbir Kapoor), a womanizing conman who falls in love with Tinni (Shraddha Kapoor) while on vacation in Spain. The movie also features Mickey's progressive mother (Dimple Kapadia) and supportive sister (Hasleen Kaur).Mickey and Tinni eventually hook up, introduce each other to their families, and the families begin preparations for their wedding. However, the movie also shows how Mickey and Tinni realize they want different things.

  5. Yeh Jawaani Hai Deewani
    • Year: 2013
    • Main Actors: Ranbir Kapoor, Deepika Padukone, Aditya Roy Kapur, Kalki Koechlin
    • Description: Yeh Jawaani Hai Deewani is a 2013 Bollywood romantic comedy that follows four friends as they grow up and navigate their relationships:
      Naina Talwar (Deepika Padukone) is a medical student who falls in love with Bunny (Ranbir Kapoor) on a train station and during a trekking trip, but doesn't tell him
      Kabir Thapar (Ranbir Kapoor) is a carefree college student who loves to travel and doesn't believe in marriage
      Aditi (Kalki Koechlin) and Avi (Aditya Roy Kapur) are Bunny's friends who explore different friendship dynamics
      The movie explores themes of romance, relationships, and friendship, and how the characters affect and change each other. It follows the characters from their carefree early 20s to their search for stability as they approach 30, and includes events like Naina taking a leap of faith with Bunny, Kabir studying abroad, and Aditi realizing Avi doesn't have feelings for her.The friends eventually drift apart but meet again at a friend's wedding.
