Introduction

Here you will find information about using special characters in HTML.

Special characters like <, >, &, and " need to be written with HTML entities to display correctly in the browser.

For example, to write "5 < 10 & 10 > 5", you should use: "5 < 10 & 10 > 5".

More Examples of Special Characters

Here are a few more examples of special characters:

  • Euro symbol: €
  • Trademark symbol: ™
  • Registered symbol: ®
  • Less than or equal to: ≤
  • Greater than or equal to: ≥
